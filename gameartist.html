AWARE

A weather awareness game where students play as emergency managers in a fictional region beset by various natural disasters. The demographic was students grades 6-10 to increase knowledge of reading weather maps, meteorology terminology, and STEM careers.

Contributions:

  • Sprite weather animations
  • Character illustrations
  • Marketing materials
  • Scene transitions
  • UI animations
